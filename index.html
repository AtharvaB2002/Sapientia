'Nullius in verba': Take Nobody's Word for it, You Idiot Fish.

You idiot fish!

Fishes were relatively earlier forms of life on earth, until in redundant terms a fish developed air-breathing mechanisms and lungs, and ventured out onto the land, that was an epocryphal moment in regards to human evolution, that fish became human (not that specific one). Millions of years later, for some reason i feel like many of us still feel like a fish which wants to venture out, but has no lungs yet. This is an attempt to prepare for the first step to becoming a human, to make lungs and take a deepest possible breath of all that sweet sweet oxygen rich cocktail.
