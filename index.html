Venture

Millions of years ago, a relatively complex creature an arthopod such as a millipede or centipede was the first living thing to walk on land, that was an epochal moment in our evolutionary history.

In our own small ways, we humans face similar moments of potential change. We stand at the edges of what we know, looking out at the unfamiliar, wondering if we dare to step forward. This blog is my attempt to take those steps. Here, I try to explore various ideas that intrigue me.

Each post is my random or structured effort to understand something new, to grow a little, and maybe to change.
